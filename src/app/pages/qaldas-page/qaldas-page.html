Who we are

At qaldas, we are driven by real-world challenges—combining deep expertise in quantum computing, cryptography, and full-stack software and hardware development to deliver solutions that matter.

We empower individuals, institutions, and businesses with cutting-edge technologies tailored to the Latin American context, while maintaining full readiness to serve international clients.

Inspired by leaders in the region like QMexico—an academic community successfully advancing quantum science and education across Mexico and Colombia —Qaldas shares their mission of democratizing access to emerging technologies, cultivating scientific literacy, and fostering a tech ecosystem that thrives with innovation and inclusion.

Our Mission

At qaldas, we believe that technology should solve meaningful problems and create opportunities for people and communities. We harness the power of quantum and classical computing, artificial intelligence, and machine learning to deliver real-world solutions through research, education, and innovation. Our mission is to democratize access to these emerging technologies, strengthen digital security through cryptography, and empower inclusive growth across Latin America and beyond.

Our Vision

To become a leading force in shaping the future of technology in Latin America by making quantum computing, artificial intelligence, and advanced cryptography accessible, inclusive, and impactful. We envision a region where communities, researchers, and industries collaborate to solve real challenges using cutting-edge tools—from classical systems to quantum—bridging global innovation with local solutions.
